Finishing touches

This includes: watermark; slight fixes; additional detail. Watermark is for obvious reasons: so other's wont steal it and claim it as theirs. Slight fixes can wary from fixing colours to whole proportions being moved around (it actually happened). I almost never added additional details, as background is usually my last stop, but it usualy is only text. It's best to wait an hour or a day before doing this step, as it allows your brain to look at the art with a new-comer look, instead of "I have watched these pixels for 5 hours straight and any mistake I see is gone behind the light", allowing you to spot the inconsistencies and mistakes.
